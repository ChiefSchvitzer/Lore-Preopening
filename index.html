The Club

Five spaces,
five brand identities

Every space has a distinct atmosphere and a distinct sponsorship character. Match the brand to the moment.

Main Sauna + Aufguss
The centerpiece. The Aufguss ceremony — towel-waving with scented water poured on rocks — is a 45-min theatrical event. Two sessions daily. The highest-premium brand window on the schedule.
Scent sponsorshipCeremony namingTowel/robe brandingRitual experience
Pool Room
Cold plunge and hot pool — the contrast therapy heart of Lore. High dwell time, high emotion. Best for hydration, recovery, and movement brands. Content-rich environment.
Hydration stationRecovery productSpace namingMovement sessions
Infrared Suite
Quieter, more intimate. Lower temperature, longer sessions. Appeals to the wellness-forward, skin-conscious crowd. Ideal for skin, beauty, and mindfulness brands with a story to tell.
Skin & beauty2-hour windowsMeditation audioSuite naming
Locker Room / Changing
High-touch, intimate, practical. Where products are actually used and noticed. A 2-hour exclusive turns this into a brand discovery moment unlike any other activation.
Amenity drops2-hour exclusivesGroomingSkin care station
Reception & Café
The welcome and recovery moment — where guests arrive, collect gift bags, and unwind. Highest visibility for signage and product. F&B brands dominate here: breakfast, matcha service, juices, supplements. The natural home for the gift bag station and the welcome brand experience.

Email cadence for partners

Presenting + Founding sponsors receive a co-branded pre-event announcement and post-event recap to the full Lore CRM list. Space + window sponsors are named in the day-of guest email and recap. Gift bag and amenity partners are credited in the recap only. Lore retains editorial control over all copy. Session passes are non-expiring, valid from May 12 public opening onwards.
