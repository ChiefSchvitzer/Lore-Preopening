About Lore

Lore — a bathing club
for serious bathers

Introducing a neighborhood bathing club called Lore. Featuring a large Finnish sauna, an intimate infrared sauna, a generous cold pool and hammam-style heated benches. Carefully considered bathing and hospitality essentials.

"Every moment at Lore is designed — arrival, heat, cold, recovery, departure. Partnership here means owning one of those moments."
The audience
Members + culture
Wellness-forward, creatively engaged, brand-literate New Yorkers. They notice what's in the changing room. They remember the scent in the sauna.
The environment
Slowed down, sensory
No phones in the bathing areas. No distraction. Guests are fully present — which means brand experiences here land differently than anywhere else.
The format
Thermal rituals
Main sauna with Aufguss ceremonies, cold plunge, hot pool, infrared suite, locker rooms, and a café — each space a distinct chapter in the guest journey.
The moment
Pre-opening weekend
Three days before anyone else gets in. The founding guest list — inner circle, press, talent, and members — represents the people who will define Lore's cultural moment.

The Spaces

Five spaces.
Five brand identities.

Every space at Lore has a distinct atmosphere and a distinct sponsorship character. The right brand in the right space creates something guests actually remember.

Main Sauna + Aufguss
The centerpiece of Lore. The Aufguss ceremony — scented water poured on rocks, distributed through towel-waving — is a 45-minute theatrical ritual. Two sessions daily. The highest-premium brand window on the schedule.
Scent sponsorshipCeremony namingRitual experienceTowel / robe branding
Pool Room
Cold plunge and hot pool — the contrast therapy heart of Lore. High dwell time, high emotion. The strongest space for hydration, recovery, and movement brands. High content potential.
Hydration stationRecovery productMovement sessionsSpace naming
Infrared Suite
Quieter, more intimate. Lower temperature, longer sessions. Draws the skin-conscious, wellness-forward crowd. Ideal for beauty, skincare, and mindfulness brands with something to say.

Brand Categories

Where your brand
belongs

We're curating a small group of partners across specific categories. Each category maps to one or more spaces and moments in the weekend program.

Scent & Fragrance
The highest-intimacy brand moment at Lore. The Aufguss ceremony is defined by scent — a fragrance brand here is central to the ritual, not peripheral to it.
Best fit: Fragrance houses, fine scent brands, botanical perfumers, essential oil brands
Skincare & Beauty
Heat opens pores. Cold closes them. Guests are acutely aware of what they're putting on their skin. Product here doesn't just get noticed — it gets used.
Best fit: Skincare, body care, clean beauty, post-heat recovery brands
F&B + Beverages
The café is the highest-traffic space in the club. Breakfast, matcha, juice, and supplement bars are all brand-ownable moments across the day.
Best fit: Functional beverages, matcha, juice, plant-based food, nutrition brands
Wellness & Nutrition
Lore's audience is actively invested in how they feel. Supplements, adaptogens, and recovery products find an unusually receptive audience here.
Best fit: Supplements, adaptogens, recovery, hormonal wellness, functional nutrition
Grooming
The locker room is one of the most intimate brand environments in any activation — and one of the least saturated. Product placed here gets picked up, used, and remembered.
Best fit: Razors, men's grooming, hair care, body wash, clean formulation brands
Apparel & Movement
Movement sessions in the pool room create natural co-branding moments for apparel and footwear brands — particularly on the high-profile Saturday program.
Best fit: Running, training, wellness apparel, performance footwear, active lifestyle brands
